06 — Key Insights & Actions
🚨
Month 1 is Make-or-Break
35.3% of all churned sellers only ever had 1 active selling month. If a seller doesn't return after their first sale, they almost never will. You need an aggressive Day 30 reactivation campaign.
📉
6 Items/Month = Churn Signal
Sellers drop to 6 items/month in their final active month. Any seller below 5 items/month for 2 consecutive months should trigger an automatic intervention or outreach.
⭐
High Performers Still Leave
375 sellers averaged 30+ items/month but still churned after ~10.6 months. This signals platform or fee dissatisfaction, not performance issues. Exit surveys are critical.
⏱
Give Sellers 6 Months to Bloom
Median time to peak is 5 months with a notable spike at month 11. Don't write off slow starters early — some take nearly a year to find their stride.
🔁
865 Sellers Came Back
855 returning sellers took a break but came back. Win-back campaigns work. Target sellers inactive for 2–3 months with a fee discount or bonus to pull them back.
